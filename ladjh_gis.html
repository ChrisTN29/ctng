Mapping Digital Access and Demographic Interactions in LA

This mapping tool provides a visualization of digital access variables, such as internet and computer availability, across LA County. It cross-represents various demographic factors to analyze how different demographics interact with access to digital technologies. This approach aligns with the foundational concepts of data justice, focusing on access disparities. While discussions around data justice have evolved to address issues like equitable representation by algorithms, hidden assumptions in models, and the implications of a data-driven economy, this map serves as an introductory resource for exploring digital justice in LA. Starting from these early themes, the LA Data Justice Hub aims to expand this tool and develop further resources and discussions to gain a detailed and practical understanding of data inequities and equitable solutions, examining how data is developed, represented, and used to empower communities.

This current iteration of the tool (V_0.0) is a prototype intended to explore design approach.
